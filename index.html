LIBRARY MANAGEMENT

The library management system allows librarians to track the availability of books, manage borrowing and return of books, and keep track of fines and fees associated with late returns. And it is designed to be user-friendly and efficient, allowing librarians to perform their duties quickly and easily.

ABOUT US

Library Manangement was developed by Vince C. Doroja that currently 3rd year student in Pamantasan ng Lungson ng Muntinlupa. The programmer are committed to provide staff with the tools they need to provide high-quality service to their students and manage the books effectively.

Features:

Our system allows librarians to catalog and organize materials quickly and easily, making it easy for patrons to find what they're looking for.

Our system streamlines the circulation process, making it easy for librarians to check books in and out and manage borrowing and returns.

Our system provides comprehensive reporting and analytics, giving librarians the data they need to make informed decisions about collection development and resource allocation.
